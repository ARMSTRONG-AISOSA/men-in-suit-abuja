Heart 2 Heart Session

This session is the heart of the meeting. It offers an open and honest environment for you to share your experiences, challenges, wisdom and victories. It’s a time and place where young men can openly discuss personal struggles, triumphs, ambition, faith and areas for growth.

Networking Session

The Networking Session provides an opportunity for you to build connections with other growth-minded Men. As discussions and networking unfold, the table will be filled with an assortment of delicious, thoughtfully prepared meals for a fine dining experience. This adds an element of elegance, oneness and community to the event as participants bond over excellent cuisine.
